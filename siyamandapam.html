About Siya Mandapam

Welcome to Siya Mandapam, the biggest wedding hall in Sub Urban Moradabad region. We specialize in superb food and advanced service to make your experience better with our premium banquet facilities.

Located at D Pratap Farmhouse, Sidawali, Kashipur Road, we offer an extensive venue with the largest capacity in the region, ensuring your celebrations are grand and memorable.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, combined with traditional hospitality, create the perfect setting for weddings, corporate events, and special celebrations.
